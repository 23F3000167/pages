Financial Math

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ \text{NPV} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1 + r)^t} - I_0 \]

Earnings per Share (EPS): \( \displaystyle EPS = \frac{\text{Net Income} - \text{Preferred Dividends}}{\text{Weighted Avg. Shares}} \)

Black–Scholes (call): \( C = S\,\Phi(d_1) - K e^{-rT}\,\Phi(d_2) \)
